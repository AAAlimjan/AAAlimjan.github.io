Quotes from Game of Thrones

Author: A Date: February 17, 2020  22:44:10 Category: TV & Movie

  • “Winter is coming” – Ned Stark
  • “The man who passes the sentence should swing the sword” – Ned Stark
  • “In my dreams, I kill him every night” – Robert Baratheon
  • “Never forget what you are. The rest of the world will not. Wear it like armor, and it can never be used to hurt you” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “All dwarves are bastards in their father’s eyes” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “The things I do for love” – Jaime Lannister
  • “There is no word for thank you in Dothraki” – Jorah Mormont
  • “And a mind needs books like a sword needs a whetstone” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “They never tell you how they all shit themselves. They don’t put that part in the songs” – Robert Baratheon
  • “Everyone who isn’t us, is an enemy” – Cersei Lannister
  • “The brave men didn’t kill dragons. The brave men rode them” – Viserys Targaryen
  • “I only know she was the one thing I ever wanted, and someone took her away from me, and seven kingdoms couldn’t fill the hole she left behind” – Robert Baratheon
  • “Brothels make a much better investment than ships, I’ve found. Whores rarely sink” – Lord Baelish
  • “Oh wait, I just realized, I don’t care” – Sansa Stark
  • “There is only one god, and his name is Death. And there is only one thing we say to Death: not today” – Syrio Forel
  • “But I grew up with soldiers. I learned how to die a long time ago” – Ned Stark
  • “You once loved a woman many years ago, but it turned out badly so you’ve never let yourself love again. Oh wait, that’s me” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “A stupid saying. What we don’t know is usually what gets us killed” – Lord Baelish
  • “You must be one of the few men in the city who isn’t a man” – Lord Baelish
  • “When the sun rises in the west, and sets in the east, then you shall return to me, my sun and stars” – Daenerys Targaryen
  • “You can’t trust anyone in King’s Landing, they’re all liars. Good liars, bad liars, one or two great liars” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “Power is power” – Cersei Lannister
  • “I’m not questioning your honor, Lord Janos. I’m denying its existence” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “Without question? No. I’d ask how much” – Bronn
  • “Power is a curious thing, my lord…Power resides where men believe it resides. It’s a trick, a shadow on the wall. And, a very small man can cast a very large shadow” – Lord Varys
  • “Shadows cannot live in the dark, Ser Davos. They are servants of light. The children of fire. And the brighter the flame, the darker they are” – Lady Melisandre
  • “There’s no cure for being a cunt” – Bronn
  • “In my family we say, “A naked man has few secrets. A flayed man none.” – Roose Bolton
  • “Loyal service means telling hard truths” – Ser Davos Seaworth
  • "Hard truths cut both ways, Ser Davos” – Stannis Baratheon
  • “We’ve had vicious kings, and we’ve had idiot kings, but I don’t know if we’ve ever been cursed with a vicious idiot for a king!” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “The more people you love, the weaker you are. You’ll do things for them that you know you shouldn’t do. You’ll act the fool to make them happy, to keep them safe. Love no one but your children; on that front a mother has no choice” – Cersei Lannister
  • “I will hurt you for this. A day will come when you think you’re safe and happy, and your joy will turn to ashes in your mouth, and you’ll know the debt is paid” – Tyrion Lannister
  • “I asked him, “How can a man be brave if he’s afraid?” “That is the only time a man can be brave”, he told me” – Robb Stark
  • “The gods have no mercy, that’s why they’re gods” – Cersei Lannister
  • “Tears aren’t a woman’s only weapon. The best one’s between your legs” – Cersei Lannister
  • “Any man dies with a clean sword, I’ll rape his fucking corpse” – The Hound
  • “The world is built by killers, so you better get used to looking at them” – The Hound
  • “Look around you. We’re all liars here – and every one of us is better than you” – Lord Baelish
  • “You will betray the men serving you, you will betray your family, you will betray everything you once held dear. And it will all be worth it” – Melisandre
